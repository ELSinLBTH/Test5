Halted Schemes Schemes

An overview of schemes that have been halted- Update from July 24.

Mill Harbour

Mill Harbour (Great Eastern Enterprise Centre) 3 Millharbour,6-8 South Quay Square

Construction has been on hold since the end of Q1 2022 and the scheme has not yet been launched. The JRL Group has erected a temporary site office here, but this will serve other JRL projects on Marsh Wall and not this site.

Poplar Business Park

Poplar Business Park - Manhattan Plaza 10 Prestons Way

Telford has delivered 120 of the 321 private units permitted on site. These completed during Q4 2017 and sold out during Q2 2019. The remaining light industrial estate has permission for 222 units, including 201 private, but Bloom and TPG Angelo Gordon has bought it with plans to retain it in industrial use.

Royal Mint Gardens

Royal Mint Gardens Chamber Street; Mansell Street

Phase 1 was DLR encapsulation works which completed in Q1 2018.

Phase 2a is 254 units in Blocks B, C and D, which completed during Q1 2020. Around 235 units had sold by the end of Q4 2021 and the developer decided to rent the rest.

Phase 2b has permission for an aparthotel and 79 private units in Block A. Application PA/15/02773 sought 196 residential units instead but was never approved. More recently, application PA/22/01284 submitted on 16/12/2022 seeks to intensify the aparthotel use. However, there would still be 79 private units in the block. Recent additions to the casefile suggest the application is still being pursued.

Poplar Transformer Station

Poplar Transformer Station Gale Street Watts Grove

Swan commenced construction during Q3 2019. The scheme is 65 units, all affordable, but work has been on hold since a fatal crane collapse on 08/07/2020.

Bow River Village

Bow River Village Hancock Road Three Mill Lane

Phase 1 is complete, sold out/rented and contained 145 private units and 35 affordable. Phase 2 is 112 units, 73 of which are private, sold out and completed during Q1 2020.

449 private units have yet to commence in future phases - Phase 3 was subject to application 19/00477/FUL for 435 units, including 224 private, but that was refused on 09/04/2021.

Aspen Way

North Quay Aspen Way

Canary Wharf Group commenced construction during Q2 2024: The overall parent consent is largely commercial but includes 702 residential units - 70% private and 30% affordable.

Union Bridge

Union Bridge (TSVC Building) Hepscott Road Rothbury Road

Construction on hold since Q4 2022.

Glacier Point

Glacier Point 472 Hackney Road, 25 Felix Street

Phase 1 was 55 units, which sold out during Q1 2018, having completed during Q3 2017. The site for the remaining 167 units in Phase 2 remains partly in use as a shabby industrial estate - there has been no further action on the planning or construction fronts since Phase 1 sold out.
